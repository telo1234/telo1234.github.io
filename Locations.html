Locations

Ashina Outskirts

The Ashina Outskirts are the initial area players encounter, filled with soldiers and wild beasts. The terrain is rugged and offers a glimpse into the declining state of the Ashina clan's territory.

Ashina Castle

Ashina Castle is the central stronghold of the Ashina clan. Its imposing walls and strategic location make it a significant challenge to infiltrate, filled with elite guards and powerful enemies.

Hirata Estate

The Hirata Estate, shown in a memory, is where Sekiro revisits his past. The estate is under attack, engulfed in flames, and provides insight into Sekiro's history and the tragic events that shaped him.

Senpou Temple, Mt. Kongo

Senpou Temple is located on Mt. Kongo, a serene yet eerie place. The temple grounds are occupied by monks who have strayed from their path, and the area hides many dark secrets and challenges.

Sunken Valley

The Sunken Valley is a perilous region characterized by steep cliffs and deep ravines. This area is home to dangerous gun-wielding enemies and the fearsome Guardian Ape.

Ashina Depths

The Ashina Depths are dark and mysterious, located deep beneath Ashina. This area is filled with poisonous swamps, hidden dangers, and the remnants of a forgotten past.

Fountainhead Palace

The Fountainhead Palace is a majestic and ethereal location. It is home to the Divine Dragon and offers breathtaking views and challenging enemies, showcasing the pinnacle of Sekiro's world.

Ashina Reservoir

The Ashina Reservoir is a crucial point in the game, where players face significant battles. The area is surrounded by water and serves as a strategic location within the Ashina Castle grounds.

Dilapidated Temple

The Dilapidated Temple serves as the player's base of operations. Here, Sekiro can rest, upgrade his abilities, and interact with important NPCs like the Sculptor and Emma.

Abandoned Dungeon

The Abandoned Dungeon is a dark and eerie area filled with undead enemies and sinister experiments. It connects various parts of the game's world and is a place of foreboding and danger.

Mibu Village

Mibu Village is a haunting and desolate place, filled with ghostly villagers and hidden secrets. This area is crucial for uncovering more about the game's lore and the mysteries of immortality.
