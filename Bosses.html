Bossses

Gyoubu Oniwa

Meet Gyoubu Masataka Oniwa, one of the formidable bosses in Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. A towering warrior mounted on a powerful warhorse, Gyoubu commands the battlefield with his immense spear, sweeping through foes with deadly precision. His booming voice and fearsome presence instill dread in even the bravest of shinobi.

Lady Butterfly

Lady Butterfly is an agile and deceptive combatant, using illusions and acrobatics to confound her opponents. Her mastery of kunai and ninja techniques makes her a deadly adversary for any who cross her path in the Hirata Estate.

Genichiro Ashina

Genichiro Ashina, the grandson of Isshin Ashina, is a relentless warrior who seeks to protect his homeland at any cost. With his deadly bow and lightning-fast sword skills, he presents a formidable challenge to any who oppose him.

Folding Screen Monkeys

The Folding Screen Monkeys are an unusual boss encounter, requiring strategy and cunning to overcome. Each monkey has unique abilities and must be defeated in a specific manner to succeed.

Guardian Ape

The Guardian Ape is a massive and ferocious beast that uses its raw strength and primal instincts to attack. Its resilience and brutal attacks make it a fearsome opponent in the depths of the Sunken Valley.

Corrupted Monk

The Corrupted Monk is a ghostly figure who wields a naginata with deadly grace. Her ethereal form and powerful attacks require precision and timing to defeat.

Headless Ape

The Headless Ape is a terrifying opponent that continues to fight even after decapitation. Its frenzied attacks and haunting roars are a true test of endurance and skill.

Emma/Isshin Ashina

Emma and Isshin Ashina are skilled warriors with deep connections to the Ashina Clan. Emma's healing abilities and Isshin's formidable swordsmanship make them a formidable pair in battle.

Great Shinobi Owl

The Great Shinobi Owl is a master shinobi and adoptive father to Sekiro. His cunning tactics and powerful ninjutsu techniques make him a formidable and deeply personal adversary.

True Corrupted Monk

The True Corrupted Monk is an enhanced version of the Corrupted Monk, with greater power and more aggressive attacks. This fight tests the player's skill and adaptability to new challenges.

Divine Dragon

The Divine Dragon is a majestic and ancient creature that resides in the Fountainhead Palace. Its otherworldly attacks and breathtaking presence make for a memorable and awe-inspiring battle.

(Owl) Father

(Owl) Father is a formidable warrior from Sekiro's past, presenting a challenging fight that demands mastery of combat mechanics. His skills and strategy are unmatched, requiring the player to be at their best.

Demon Of Hatred

The Demon of Hatred is a tragic figure consumed by rage and suffering. Its massive size and fiery attacks make it a daunting opponent that embodies the destructive power of hatred.

Isshin, The Sword Saint

Isshin, The Sword Saint, is the final boss of Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. His unparalleled swordsmanship and mastery of combat make him the ultimate test of the player's skill and perseverance.

Inner Genichiro

Inner Genichiro represents a more powerful and refined version of Genichiro Ashina. His enhanced abilities and relentless attacks provide a greater challenge for players seeking to test their limits.

Inner Father

Inner Father is a more formidable version of (Owl) Father, with enhanced techniques and greater power. This fight demands exceptional skill and precision to overcome.

Inner Isshin

Inner Isshin is a more challenging version of Isshin, The Sword Saint, featuring increased speed and more aggressive attacks. This battle is the ultimate test of the player's abilities and determination.
